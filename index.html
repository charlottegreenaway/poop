Welcome!

I’m Helen, I would like to invite you to experience my work as a breathing space that will fill you and inspire you with pattern, vibrant colour and unique shapes. My art dolls and fusion quilts are in homes and galleries around the world. Example pieces are on my Shop page where there is a link to my shop. You can browse to your hearts content. Please don’t hesitate to contact me with any questions you may have. I have work for sale and am very happy to undertake commissions.
Pieces range from £20 for small stitched pieces to £1,500 for full size quilts and figurative sculptures.
I accept PayPal or Stripe.

In my workshop series you can explore the techniques that I have developed and adapted. The workshops and courses encourage you to play, explore, learn, create and celebrate your own authentic voice. I have worked with complete beginners and professional artists. Each individual takes what resonates with them to develop their own work.


There are 8 short half day workshops –
  • 7 doll / soft sculpture.
  • 1 quilting technique – Kwandi.


A 4 week course –
  • Fusion quilts where you will be guided through, my techniques and processes to create a full journal. This course is dyslexia and disability friendly – no maths and go crawling around on the floor with yards of fabric to control.


An annual holiday every Autumn / Fall and In March each year a holiday cantered around the Sitges Patchwork festival in Spain.
  • The Embodied stitch holiday, is a week long hand stitching retreat for you to dive deep into your own work.
  • Sitges Patchwork Festival, 2 days at the festival and 5 days hand stitching and soaking up Spanish culture.


I am happy to work with groups and guilds. Minimum 6 participants.
